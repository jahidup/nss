Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

Sankalp Digital Pathshala operates this web portal to outline classroom courses, host AI study helpers, and register class inquiries. We respect individual information privacy standards.

1. Data Collection

We collect details when you fill out enrollment registration forms, contact inquires desks, or request counselor calls inside the chatbot panels. Collected fields include student name, target class, email ID, mobile numbers, city and parent details.

2. Use of Information

Information is utilized to route academic counselor calls, verify student identities for marksheets lookup, and evaluate leads scoring profiles using AI models. We do not transfer records databases to external marketing groups.

3. Session Security

Admin login portals utilize JWT authentication tokens protected under httpOnly parameters to avoid browser access vulnerability breaches. We utilize SSL encryptions across database pipelines.
