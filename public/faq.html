FAQ Help Portal

Search our comprehensive database for answers to common questions about admissions, AI tools, and test series schedules.

Sankalp Digital Pathshala is a premium offline coaching academy based in New Delhi, offering structured study programs for school boards (6th-12th) and competitive examinations (JEE, NEET, Olympiads), reinforced by modern AI-based learning helpers.

Students can upload images of questions, text, or PDF worksheets to our portal. The backend queries Google Gemini API to analyze the details and returns comprehensive step-by-step solutions instantly. These tools are completely free to use on our portal without registration.

We do not run hostel buildings directly. However, we have tie-ups with verified nearby student PG residencies that offer clean food, study environments, and transport utilities for outer-state students.

Visit our Results page, input the student's unique Registration Number (issued by the admin desk) and Date of Birth. If the controller has published the sheet, it will generate a verified official marksheet ready for print.

"Rojgaar Buddy" is our career integration framework where senior class students receive expert guidance, alignment advice on modern engineering domains (AI, cloud, data science), and vocational skills pathways to prepare them for practical professions early.
