Our Teaching Philosophy

We believe in building active reasoning habits rather than focusing purely on rote learning formulas. Our instructors split lectures into logical modules:

  • First 30m: Core concept explanation & real-world analogies.
  • Next 30m: Interactive classroom problem-solving.
  • Last 15m: Quick doubt check and homework sheet assignment.
